Game Info

You play as the X's and the computer is the O's. Select the square you want to put your X into by clicking them. You cannot occupy a square that is already occupied. The first player to get three squares in a row wins.

Wanna A Game? Press button or "Space"!

Or press "Space" to start/restart a new game
